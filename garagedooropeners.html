GARAGE DOOR OPENERS

Garage door openers not only provide convenience for the homeowner. They also provide security. The garage door is the largest door or entryway into your home, and as such, needs to be kept secure at all times. A high quality, expertly installed garage door opener is the best way to safeguard your home, and give you and your family the peace of mind you deserve! When choosing a garage door opener, there are a few factors to keep in mind, such as horsepower, headroom, drive system, etc. And there are many options available to suit all of your needs! Most models come standard now with internet capability, which allows you to monitor, connect to and control your garage door opener and lights right from your internet-enabled smartphone, tablet or computer! Another popular option is battery back-up, which allows you to continue using your garage door, in the event of a power outage. Boskom, Inc. repairs all makes and models of openers.
