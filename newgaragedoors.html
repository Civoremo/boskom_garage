NEW GARAGE DOORS

Choosing the right door for your home can sometimes be a bit overwhelming, with all of the choices available to you. A garage door is such a large part of your homes overall look and curb appeal, so you want to make sure and select a door that will give you the biggest return on your investment! At Boskom, Inc. Garage Doors, every customer is given personalized service!

Listed below are 5 easy steps, to help get you started.

1. Type of Garage Door
Steel Garage Door

Flush insulated steel garage doors in a durable, natural looking, stained wood Ultra-Grain paint finish to complement both traditional and contemporary home styles.

Wood Garage Door

Authentic handcrafted wood carriage house garage doors are insulated to provide beauty, energy efficiency and strength. Custom designs and wood species are available.

Fiberglass Garage Door

Artfully molded wood-grain fiberglass surface concealing durable steel construction. Fiberglass is as close as you can get to the warmth and beauty of wood with less maintenance.

Aluminum Garage Door

Aluminum doors made of glass and aluminum complement your home’s clean, modern look. The large full view glass panels beautifully fuse indoor and outdoor spaces.

2. Insulated vs Non-Insulated
Insulated Door

Double-layer steel doors have a galvanized steel skin on the outside with a thick layer of either polystyrene or polyurethane as a backer. The backer provides soundproofing and additional insulating value to the door.

Non-Insulated Doors

Single-layer doors are stamped from a single sheet of galvanized steel. These are usually the most economical of all steel doors. These doors are a single layer and do not have any insulation.
